About Rust

Rust is a online multiplayer survival game, developed by FacePunch studios. In this game, not only are you survivng against the environment, you are in a constant struggle against the other players, for who can have the best loot. The aim is to construct a strong base, then obtain loot by crafting or through PVP, and then raid other players bases to take the loot for yourself. You are playing on a large island map, which consists of large monuments and biomes, with constant events happening giving you the opportunity to get even more loot
