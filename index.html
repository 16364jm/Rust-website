About Rust

Rust is a online multiplayer survival game, developed by FacePunch studios. In this game, not only are you survivng against the environment, you are in a constant struggle against the other players, for who can have the best loot. The aim is to construct a strong base, then obtain loot by crafting or through PVP, and then raid other players bases to take the loot for yourself. You are playing on a large island map, which consists of large monuments and biomes, with constant events happening giving you the opportunity to get even more loot.

.


Rust was released on the 11th of december in 2013, which is now called Rust Legacy, as this old version of Rust got outdated, causing the developers to swap to a experimental build, the game we play today. Rust was initially created as a clone to the common game, DayZ, with crafting elements of minecraft mixed in. The game was very popular when it first came out, as in its first two weeks it sold over 150,000 copie sof the game, with over 1 million copies sold by the end of the early access. There was mixed reviews, but the overall view of this game was positive.
